28 · 03 · 09
It wasn't supposed to happen that fast. But before the day had even decided what it was going to be — lips. The first real thing. And then the words that followed, whispered like they'd been rehearsed in another life: I'm not going anywhere. Not ever.
// first meeting. first kiss. first promise that meant it.
23 — 24 · 05 · 25
The movie was irrelevant. Two days the world shrank to just the two of them — every smooch a sentence, every kiss a conversation finished without words. They felt each other the way you feel something you've wanted so long you've forgotten what waiting felt like.
// warmth. closeness. love that didn't stop at the credits.
21 · 08 · 25
Mud walls that held the heat just right. A bathtub full of warm water and rose petals floating like they'd always been there. A tiny cycle hung above — an odd, perfect thing. The round mirror on the wall watched them forget the city, the calendar, everything outside that room. It was the first morning of a life chosen on purpose.
// roses in the water. them in each other. nothing else.
28 · 10 · 25
Same mud walls. Same petals on the water. The little cycle still hung, swaying slightly. The round mirror still watched. But this time — the seal broke. Everything that had been patiently, quietly waiting came all at once: all the love, all the closeness, all the warmth that only two people who have truly chosen each other can make.
// they forgot the world. the world could wait.
